AU Encounter Record

The Sparked FHIR Accelerator is meeting late February for a workshop to ask its
stakeholders about the possibility of a FHIR-ized “AU Encounter Record.”

Some of the workshop agenda items include:

• When is an Encounter Record created and how is it used?
•Industry examples of encounter handover records (pharmacy meds review,
virtual / urgent care, ambulance visit

• Is AU Encounter Record a document? Are the sections the similar? Is anything
mandatory? Is it attested/signed?
• Is AU Encounter Record a new implementation guide? Are there additional
dependencies on AU CDI or AU Base/Core?

https://sparked.csiro.au/index.php/events-home/sparked-events/sparked-community-co-
design-workshops-25-26-february-2026-in-person-hobart/

ASTP/ONC can research this to see if this could be a movement for FHIR Documents
or FHIR Clinical Documents to move to a FHIR based US Encounter Record in the
future.
