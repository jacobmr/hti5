FROM: Rohan Sharma, Aspen Institute Civic AI Leader

Attached is a technical comment regarding the ASTP/ONC Deregulatory Actions to Unleash Prosperity (RIN 0955-AA09).

This response identifies critical implementation vulnerabilities in the proposed removal of privacy and security certification criteria (45 CFR 170.315(d)) and the definitions of "market rate" within the Information Blocking regulations. It proposes specific mechanisms to maintain HIPAA-aligned technical baselines and ensure AI model transparency without imposing undue regulatory friction.
