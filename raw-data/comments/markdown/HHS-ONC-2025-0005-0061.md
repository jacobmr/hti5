Electronic PDFs, faxes, electronic faxes, JPEGs, and TIFs shall never substitute for standards electronic transactions or structured EHI exchange.
Entities that only send electronic PDFs, electronic faxes, faxes, JPEGs, or TIF files are committing information blocking.

Standards based machine readable formats exchanged via standards based APIs shall be the required method for the electronic exchange of health information.
