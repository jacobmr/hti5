CSV files and spreadsheets shall never substitute for standards based electronic transactions or structured EHI exchange.
Entities that attempt to exchange with CSV files or spreadsheets without standards based APIs are committing information blocking.

Standards based machine readable formats via standards based APIs shall be the required method for the electronic exchange of health information.
