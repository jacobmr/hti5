Although I applaud the use of AI to synthesize new forms of proteins, which is a great medical advancement, I think the use of AI to provide psychological services is dangerous in the extreme. A psychologically-focused AI misused would greatly exacerbate public unrest and psychological issues, as well as damaging public trust that psychology is a useful tool to help people heal mental health issues. Additionally, the profit motive of a private company marketing AI software cannot be rationalized with a physician&rsquo;s duty of care towards their patients. This is not a new piece of medical equipment, it is a robot that is designed to manipulate text to create coherent sentences. It does not reason or think in any way we imagine it, and cannot be adequately used to diagnose issues patients or doctors might seek its input on. It is a text generator which makes sense more often than not, not a thinking machine.
