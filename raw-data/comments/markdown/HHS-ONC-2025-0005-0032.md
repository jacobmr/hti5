Hello, I am an attorney from Georgia, and I represent patients and families in serious and catastrophic medical malpractice cases. In over 20 years of experience both on the patient side and representing hospitals and doctors, unfortunately, it is far too common for healthcare providers to alter the medical record after a catastrophic adverse event. Key facts, details, and information are routinely deleted and changed, and the facts of what occurred are actively covered-up in many cases. With the updated changes to Information Blocking, it is critical to protect patients (i.e. ALL of us) from not only an adverse event, but from efforts to hide, misuse, and abuse the EHR to cover-up preventable medical errors. Please ensure that the integrity of the medical record is protected and the information blocking revisions continue to impose serious penalties on facilities and providers who refuse to produce this information that belongs to the patient.
