The remaining US states that are not live on FHIR APIs need acceleration to adopt and align with the latest FHIR IGs at scale (such as the Da Vinci FHIR IGs, CARIN Alliance FHIR IGs, Gravity Project FHIR IGs) to catalyze nationwide interoperability in Medicaid interoperability infrastructure.

The remaining US territories that are not live on FHIR APIs will also need acceleration to adopt and align with the latest FHIR IGs at scale.

CMS and ASTP/ONC need to monitor FHIR IG adoption by US states and territories.

Some of the potential areas can include:
