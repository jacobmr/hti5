Mosaic Life Tech submits comments on the AI governance implications of HTI-5. We support ASTP/ONC's deregulatory objectives and FHIR-forward direction. Our comments address the accountability shift created by removing AI model card requirements, the governance readiness gap for autonomous AI-mediated access to EHI, and the need for ASTP/ONC to signal that reduced federal certification does not reduce organizational accountability.
