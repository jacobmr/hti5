Dear Office of the Assistant Secretary for Technology Policy / Office of the National Coordinator for Health Information Technology,
I am submitting this comment as an independent researcher in response to the above-referenced proposed rule published December 29, 2025. My comment is accompanied by an attached academic working paper:
Interoperability Agentic Gen AI for API-Driven Health IT: Advancing the Modernized US ONC Framework
Satyadhar Joshi (February 2026)
This paper analyzes the proposed rule's deregulatory provisions from the perspective of agentic generative AI systems, FHIR-based API interoperability, and AI governance. It draws on over 100 academic, industry, and policy sources to argue that while the rule's FHIR-forward strategy creates essential technical conditions for autonomous AI in healthcare, certain deregulatory actions &mdash; particularly the removal of AI transparency mandates and privacy/security certification criteria &mdash; create governance gaps that warrant complementary policy measures.
