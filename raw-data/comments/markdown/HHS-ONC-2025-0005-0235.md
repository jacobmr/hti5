I support efforts to reduce unnecessary administrative burden and improve real-world interoperability. Please ensure any deregulatory actions do not weaken patient privacy and transparency, and that outcomes remain measurable (e.g., consistent, usable access to health data in practice&mdash;not just &ldquo;paper interoperability&rdquo;). I also encourage attention to implementation impacts on small clinics and safety-net providers so changes don&rsquo;t widen inequities. Thank you for considering these points.
