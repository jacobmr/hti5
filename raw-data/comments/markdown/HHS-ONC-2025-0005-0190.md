I understand that HHS has proposed a rule (HTI-5) that would eliminate more than half of the current certification requirements for electronic health record systems, removing federal guardrails before updating the standards that ensure medical records are reliable. That puts the cart before the horse. Audit trails are foundational to patient safety, fraud prevention, and accountability in litigation. Standards must be modernized&mdash;especially to address AI-generated content, interoperability, metadata transparency, and data retention&mdash;before certification protections are reduced.

Mark Smith
Cincinnati, OH
