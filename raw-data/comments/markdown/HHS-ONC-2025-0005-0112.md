Drummond Group respectfully submits the following comments on the Proposed Rule for Health Technology for Interoperability and Modernization (HTI-5), specifically addressing concerns regarding the removal and modification of certified criteria.

As an NVLAP and ANAB accredited test laboratory and certification body administering the ONC Health IT Certification Program, Drummond Group has direct experience in assessing and validating health IT products against established certification standards. The proposed changes to certification criteria carry significant implications for the certification process and the assurance it provides to the health IT ecosystem.

This submission focuses on the operational and systemic impacts of the proposed criterion removals and their effects on certification rigor and interoperability assurance.

We are grateful for the opportunity to provide these comments.
