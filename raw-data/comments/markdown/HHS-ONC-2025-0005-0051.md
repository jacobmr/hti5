I am an attorney practicing exclusively in representing victims of medical negligence. The proposed rule deregulating regulations regarding Health information technology standards, implementation specifications, and certification criteria and certification programs for health information technology, and information blocking would hurt victims of medical negligence and help the wrongdoers who commit malpractice. Any case I work on involves obtaining the complete medical record. In every case currently, we have to fight to get complete records. I encourage you to focus rule making on helping victims instead of helping wrongdoers.
