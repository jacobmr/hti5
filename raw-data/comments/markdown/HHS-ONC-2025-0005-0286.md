NE has serious concerns about making eCR certification criteria to a functional requirement because this change would undermine progress toward standardized, automated public health reporting and impose significant new burdens on our public health agency and local health departments.

ASTP/ONC&rsquo;s desire to move towards FHIR-based standards is also an area of apprehension for us. Our state and local health agencies do not currently have the technical infrastructure or a workforce with expertise in consuming HL7 FHIR and would need additional staff, significant training and/or upskilling, and resources to establish FHIR capability. This would be an extremely difficult step for us to undertake and could impact public health response.
