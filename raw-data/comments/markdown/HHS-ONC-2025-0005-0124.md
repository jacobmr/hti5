The proposed regulations would not be helpful and would hurt many individuals within our country. Medical records are not certified many times even though requests are made for certification. The audit trail documents often is not provided or when it is provided, it is in a useable form. The audit trail data needs to be more strongly regulated and mandated to be produced at the time of the medical record disclosures.
