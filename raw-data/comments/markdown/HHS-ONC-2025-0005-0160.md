As a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with a specialization in providing affirming mental health care for transgender, non-binary and gender diverse adolescents and adults I write to implore the HHS's ONC to continue to require the collection of patient name to use, gender identity, pronouns and sex parameters. These fields ensure that healthcare providers are able to provide build trust with patients and provide the best care possible. Removing these fields will strengthen formidable, existing barriers to care for transgender, intersex and non-binary patients. Specifically failing to collect the aforementioned data about patients will increase experiences of fear and mistrust of providers, disrespect from providers and mistreatment within the medical system. Transgender, intersex and non-binary patients deserve quality care, rolling back these data fields will make that harder to achieve
