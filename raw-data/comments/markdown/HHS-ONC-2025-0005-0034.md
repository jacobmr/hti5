As a person who has worked in health care administration for more than a year, I am appalled that HHS would consider deregulating information technology standards, implementation specifications, and certification criteria and certification programs for health information technology, and information blocking at this time. This proposed deregulation places more of a financial burden on health institutions and states while potentially allowing for-profit companies to run amok without commonsense regulations that protect patients and health care workers.
