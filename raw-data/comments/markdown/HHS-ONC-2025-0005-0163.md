Dear Secretary Kennedy,

The American Pharmacists Association (APhA) appreciates the opportunity to provide comments on the &ldquo;Health Data, Technology, and Interoperability: ASTP/ONC Deregulatory Actions To Unleash Prosperity,&rdquo; proposed rule. APhA has provided multiple comments recommending ASTP/ONC take action to remove regulatory barriers for pharmacists and pharmacies to provide care, as Dr. Oz recently stated, &ldquo;at the height of their licensure.&rdquo; Dr. Oz also asked, &ldquo;Why don&rsquo;t we use pharmacists better?&rdquo;

APhA represents pharmacists, student pharmacists, and pharmacy technicians in all practice settings, including but not limited to community pharmacies, hospitals, long-term care facilities, specialty pharmacies, community health centers, physician offices, ambulatory clinics, managed care organizations, hospice settings, and government facilities. Our members strive to improve medication use, advance patient care, and enhance public health.

Full comments are attached.
