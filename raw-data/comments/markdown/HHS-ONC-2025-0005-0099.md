# Comment: HHS-ONC-2025-0005-0099

**Commenter:** Joseph Hartle

**Posted:** 2026-02-25T05:00:00Z

**Source:** https://www.regulations.gov/comment/HHS-ONC-2025-0005-0099

It is vastly important to ensure the protection of patients and to ensure the integrity of a patient's medical record and health information that HHS update and strengthen the rules and regulations regarding audit trails and preservation standards before implementing this deregulation or any deregulation of the standards. A patient must have confidence that what is contained in their medical record is a true and accurate record of their diagnoses, treatment, vitals, and actions taken. The patient also needs to know anytime that anyone accesses their record, the reason for the access, and the actions that the person took, such as additions, deletions, amendments, etc. Stronger rules and regulations are needed to ensure this integrity and trust. Thank you.
