Please see the attached 2-page comment letter regarding &ldquo;Health Data, Technology, and Interoperability: ASTP/ONC Deregulatory Actions To Unleash Prosperity&rdquo; (Docket ID: HHS-ONC-2025-0005; RIN: 0955-AA09; FR Doc. 2025-23896).

Summary: I support HTI-5 burden reduction but urge ASTP/ONC to preserve a minimal Predictive DSI baseline by replacing the proposed removal of 45 CFR 170.315(b)(11)(iv)-(vi) with a requirement that certified Health IT Modules support an exportable, machine-readable Evidence Artifact for each Predictive DSI (intended use, performance/limitations, monitoring/update cadence, and risk-governance attestation), without requiring raw training data, PHI, or disclosure of trade secrets/confidential commercial information.
