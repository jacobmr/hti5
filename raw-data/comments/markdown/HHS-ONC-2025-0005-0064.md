This is the updated version of an earlier comment:

Electronic PDFs, faxes, electronic faxes, JPEGs, and TIFs shall never substitute for standards based electronic transactions or structured EHI exchange.

ASTP/ONC and CMS have the opportunity to recommend the removal of the following as HIPAA electronic substitutes: electronic PDFs, faxes, electronic faxes, JPEGs, TIFs, CSVs, and spreadsheets.

Entities that only send electronic PDFs, electronic faxes, faxes, JPEGs, or TIF files are committing information blocking.
Standards based machine readable formats via standards based APIs shall be the required method for the electronic exchange of health information.

Health systems and hospitals who commit repeated attempts to exchange data via PDFs, faxes, JPEGs or TIFs should be subject to review by the Joint Commission.
