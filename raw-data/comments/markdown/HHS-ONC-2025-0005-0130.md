Dr. Keane,

Vega Health appreciates the opportunity to provide comments to the Assistant Secretary for Technology Policy&rsquo;s/Office of the National Coordinator for Health IT&rsquo;s (ASTP/ONC&rsquo;s) Proposed Rule regarding deregulatory actions under the 21st Century Cures Act to unleash prosperity. Vega Health supports the Administration&rsquo;s efforts to foster the ability of innovative new market entrants to compete on a level playing field in the healthcare industry, and to accelerate adoption of effective AI solutions by health systems of all sizes. In our detailed comments below, Vega Health will focus on the last mile between regulatory intent and real-world barriers to integration of new technology into the healthcare ecosystem. We specifically urge ASTP/ONC to consider the following:

&bull;Information blocking by industry incumbents remains a significant barrier to the success of new market entrants&rsquo; ability to serve providers and health systems;
&bull;The growth of AI-native healthcare companies will only increase the technical demands placed on FHIR APIs; and
&bull;The elimination of federally mandated model cards and equivalents will expand the need for companies that provide transparent, objective evaluation of AI solutions for health systems and users.
